Welcome to THE ANNA SEVA FAMILY!

Just as one of the Sanskrit saying goes " वृथा वृष्टिः समुद्रेषु, वृथा तृप्तेषु भोजनम् । वृथा दानं धनाढ्येषु वृथा दीपो दिवापि च ॥ "which means "rain in ocean is waste, food for the already-fed is waste | charity to the opulent is waste, and lamp, even in daytime, is waste ||"
There are many people who are in the need of food and are not able to get that. We have introduced Anna Seva with an objective to help those ppl fill the voids of their stomach and not go to bed without having a sense of fulfilment every single day. Our objective ,therefore , needs your support.
